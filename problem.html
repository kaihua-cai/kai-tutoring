Problem No. 1

Use +, -, *, /, (, and ) on each of the following four integers to produce 24

  • 3, 3, 7, 7
  • 4, 4, 7, 7
  • 4, 4, 10, 10

For example, for 1,5,5,5, the answer is (5 - 1/5) * 5 = 24
